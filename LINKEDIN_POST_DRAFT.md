# LinkedIn Post Draft - AI Resume Analyzer Launch

## Post Version 1 (Professional)

🚀 **Excited to introduce: AI Resume Analyzer** 🚀

I've built an elegant web application that helps job seekers optimize their resumes for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). 

**What it does:**
✅ **ATS Compatibility Scoring** — Get instant feedback on how well your resume is optimized for ATS systems
✅ **Keyword Matching** — See exactly which job requirements you match and which keywords to add
✅ **AI-Powered Suggestions** — Receive actionable, specific recommendations to improve your resume
✅ **Analysis History** — Track all your past analyses and monitor improvements over time

**The Problem:**
Many talented professionals have their resumes rejected by ATS systems before a human ever sees them. Formatting issues, missing keywords, and structural problems can silently eliminate your application.

**The Solution:**
AI Resume Analyzer uses advanced AI to analyze your resume against job descriptions, providing clear, actionable insights to increase your chances of getting past the ATS and into a recruiter's hands.

**Built with:**
- React 19 + TypeScript for a premium, responsive UI
- Express.js backend with tRPC for type-safe APIs
- Manus LLM for intelligent analysis
- Secure cloud storage for your resume files
